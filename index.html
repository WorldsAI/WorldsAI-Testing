What is Worlds.ai?

It's a visual game where you control a character by typing actions. You can create your own world or jump into one that's already made. Type what your character does (like “I enter the cave” or “She runs down the path”) and the app generates an image based on it.

Then you type the next action. And repeat.

Quick notes

  • Best for exploration and action, not great for dialogue.
  • Navigation is done through the sidebar on the left.
  • Public worlds are on the Home page, and your worlds are on the Play page. Click one to play.

Troubleshooting

  • If sign-in or loading fails, try refreshing or restarting.
  • If you see a “moderation blocked” error after entering an action, that’s an issue with the image AI being too strict. It’s known and probably something only the provider can fix.
