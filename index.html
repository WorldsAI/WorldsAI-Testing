Welcome to Worlds.ai

This is a browser-based AI storytelling app. You create or explore fantasy worlds, then guide your character by typing in actions — and the world visually evolves with AI-generated images.

👉 Launch the app

How it works

  • Create or select a world from the home screen.
  • Type your character’s next action (e.g. “I enter the cave” or “She swings her sword at the ogre”).
  • Wait a moment for the image to update.
  • Repeat and enjoy the evolving story.

Tips

  • Best suited for exploration and action; dialogue is limited for now.
  • If sign-in fails, refresh the page — that usually fixes it.
  • Public worlds are on the “Home” screen. Your own are under “Play.”
  • Worlds take about 30 seconds to generate when first created.
  • Errors with “moderation blocked” are known and being investigated — they’re likely service-side issues.
